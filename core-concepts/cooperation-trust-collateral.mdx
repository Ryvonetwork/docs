---
title: "Cooperation, Trust, And Collateral"
description: "The scaling levers Agon treats as first-class."
---

Agon does not assume every machine-payment relationship looks the same.

Some counterparties will:

- lock collateral
- rely on trust and reputation
- use a mix of collateral plus deferred settlement

That is why the protocol is best framed as a **cooperative clearing layer**. It gives repeated relationships the tools to choose how much they want to pay in:

- immediacy
- trust minimization
- capital lockup
- settlement frequency

The goal is not to eliminate trust. The goal is to make it an explicit scaling lever.
