---
title: "Participants"
description: "Permanent participant identities in Agon V4."
---

Participants are permanent protocol identities.

Each participant account binds one wallet to one stable `participant_id`. That means:

- the ID does not recycle through close/reopen flows
- offchain systems can treat `wallet -> participant_id` as stable
- identity reset requires a new wallet, not a protocol-level account recycle

This simplifies both replay protection and long-lived reputation.
