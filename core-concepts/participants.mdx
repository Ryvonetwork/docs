---
title: "Participants"
description: "Permanent participant identities in Agon V4."
---

Participants are permanent protocol identities.

Each participant account binds one wallet to one stable `participant_id`. That mapping does not recycle through close and reopen flows, which means offchain systems can treat `wallet -> participant_id` as stable for the lifetime of the deployment. If an operator wants a new identity, it uses a new wallet rather than trying to recycle protocol state.

This makes replay protection simpler and gives long-lived integrations a stable identifier to build around.
