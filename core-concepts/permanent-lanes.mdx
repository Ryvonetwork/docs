---
title: "Permanent Lanes"
description: "Why Agon V4 uses permanent directed lanes."
---

Agon V4 removed closable/reopenable payment lanes.

Each directed lane is permanent for the lifetime of the deployment and is keyed by:

- `payer_id`
- `payee_id`
- `token_id`

This change simplified the protocol in three important ways:

- no close/reopen replay boundary
- no `lane_generation`
- no lane lifecycle rent-reclaim complexity

The tradeoff is straightforward: Agon keeps more long-lived state onchain in exchange for a much cleaner settlement model.
