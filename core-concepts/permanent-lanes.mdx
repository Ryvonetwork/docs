---
title: "Permanent Lanes"
description: "Why Agon V4 uses permanent directed lanes."
---

Agon V4 removed closable and reopenable payment lanes.

Each directed lane is permanent for the lifetime of the deployment and is keyed by `payer_id`, `payee_id`, and `token_id`. Its cumulative settled amount only moves forward.

This design removes the old close and reopen replay boundary, eliminates `lane_generation`, and makes settlement semantics much easier to reason about. The tradeoff is that the protocol keeps more long-lived state onchain in exchange for a cleaner model and more stable offchain integration.
