---
title: "Latest Commitment Settlement"
description: "One lane, one latest cumulative state, one settlement transaction."
---

The direct path is the simplest Agon primitive.

The payer signs `agon-cmt-v4` to authorize a lane up to a cumulative amount. The payee only needs the latest signed state. Intermediate states are useful for metering and local accounting, but they do not all need to be settled.

This means many offchain payments on the same lane can collapse into one onchain action. The protocol only cares about forward movement from the current settled cumulative amount to a newer cumulative amount.
