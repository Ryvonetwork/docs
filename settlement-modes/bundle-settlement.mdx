---
title: "Bundle Settlement"
description: "Many unilateral commitments for one payee can settle in bundles."
---

Bundle settlement keeps the signed primitive simple.

Agon does **not** invent a separate bundle message type. Instead, one transaction verifies many independent `agon-cmt-v4` messages for one payee and advances many lanes together.

That means:

- buyers keep signing the same unilateral commitment format
- the payee gets transaction compression
- the protocol keeps the message surface compact
