---
title: "Bundle Settlement"
description: "Many unilateral commitments for one payee can settle in bundles."
---

Bundle settlement keeps the signed primitive simple.

Agon does not introduce a separate bundle message type. Instead, one transaction verifies many independent `agon-cmt-v4` messages for one payee and advances many lanes together.

That design keeps buyer-side signing uniform, gives the payee transaction compression, and avoids expanding the protocol surface with a second unilateral message family.
