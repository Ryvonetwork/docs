Agon publishes two benchmark families.

## 1. Live Devnet Benchmarks

These come from the protocol-only devnet demo. They capture the actual scenario shape, explorer links, serialized transaction bytes where measured, and settlement compression against an exact-payment baseline.

## 2. Synthetic Capacity Benchmarks

These come from deterministic sizing scripts. They measure unilateral message density, bundle settlement limits, cooperative clearing-round headroom, and projected BLS headroom.

The generated pages in this section are built from committed JSON snapshots rather than hand-maintained prose. That keeps the published tables tied to reproducible artifacts.
