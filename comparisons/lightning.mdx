---
title: "Against Lightning"
description: "How Agon's cooperative-clearing model differs from Lightning's routing model."
---

Lightning and Agon both move authorization offchain, but they scale in different ways.

Lightning scales through:

- routing
- channel liquidity
- path-dependent forwarding fees

Agon scales through:

- cumulative obligations
- payee-side bundling
- cooperative clearing

Lightning is stronger when the goal is open, trust-minimized internet money.  
Agon is stronger when the goal is repeated machine-payment relationships that want to compress settlement rather than price every hop.
