title: "V4 Overview"
description: "The shortest practical overview of Agon Protocol V4."
---

Agon Protocol V4 has a simple external model.

Each wallet registers once as a permanent participant and receives a stable `participant_id` for the lifetime of the deployment. Participants deposit allowlisted tokens into protocol balances. When one participant pays another, they create a permanent directed lane keyed by `payer_id`, `payee_id`, and `token_id`. The payer can lock funds to collateralize that lane, and the parties exchange signed V4 messages offchain as obligations change.

V4 uses two message formats. `agon-cmt-v4` authorizes a cumulative amount for one lane. `agon-round-v4` lets a group of participants sign one shared clearing round that advances many lane targets at once.

Those messages feed three settlement paths. The simplest path settles the latest commitment for one lane. Bundle settlement advances many lanes for one payee in one transaction. Cooperative clearing rounds advance many lanes together and apply only the residual balance changes that remain after netting.
